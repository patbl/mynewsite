Errors in the Alfred Edition of Grieg's Piano Sonata in E Minor, Op. 7 (Edited by Maurice Hinson)



Measure Description
I. Allegro moderato
3 The rhythm in the second beat in the right hand should be dotted-eight, sixteenth.
94 The last note in the left hand should be an eighth note.
99 On the last beat, there should be an eighth rest in the bottom voice of the left hand after the quarter note.
213 The first chord in the right hand should have an E at the bottom (i.e., add an E an octave below the one that's printed)
II. Andante molto
32 On the third beat in the right hand, a sixteenth note should precede the five sixteenth notes.
III. Alla menuetto, ma poco più lento
48 The bottom note in the right hand on beat 1 should be a G sharp, not an A.
75 On beat 1 in the right hand, the third triplet should be a G, not an F sharp.
IV. Molto allegro
74 On beat 2 in the right hand, there should be a quarter-note octave on D (the lower note of which being immediately above middle C).
94 The fp should be first beat of the measure, not the second.
210 On beat 1 of the right hand, there should be an E below the A.
293 The fp should be first beat of the measure, not the second.
335 The third G sharp in the right hand should be an E.
