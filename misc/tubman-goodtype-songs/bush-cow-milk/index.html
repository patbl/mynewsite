Bush Cow Milk


Howard Benedict Hayes, to whom Parker dedicates this song, was "a musical genius, a great songwriter and performer, though blind from birth." According to Merriam-Webster's online dictionary a bush cow is either a short-horned buffalo of West Africa (Syncerus nanus) or a tapir.

"Bush Cow Milk," by the late Prof. Howard Benedict Hayes, rearranged, played, and sung by Congress-Woman Jackson Parker. Dedicated to the three brothers, William, Frank, and Steve Talbot.
